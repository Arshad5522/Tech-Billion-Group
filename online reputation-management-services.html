    OFF PAGE OPTIMIZATION:

    Search engine optimization (SEO) famously consists of multiple qualities, and one of the most important is off-page Optimization. Often pronounced “off-page Search engine optimization(SEO)” refers to actions taken outside of your own website to improve the position of your rankings within search engine results pages (SERPs). It is very valuable because it tells the search engines that your website is important than others on the web.

    Off-page, SEO gives a very good indication of how the other websites and users perceive the particular website. Many people associate off-page SEO with link building only, but it goes way beyond that. In a more clear way, if you write a guest post for another blog or leave a comment, it counts as off-page site promotion.

    Off-page SEO refers to all the measures that can be taken outside of the main website in order to improve its position in search engine rankings. For e.g - if you’ve got a lot of high-quality backlinks (incoming links) pointing to your pages, search engines will assume that you’ve got great content.
